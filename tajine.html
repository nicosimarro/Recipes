tajine with vegetables

It is a powdered seasoning made with a simple mixture of chili, lemon and sea salt. tajine enhances the flavor of all your favorite foods, fruits and vegetables. Makes everything good taste even better

Description

It is a traditional dish of Moroccan cuisine of Berber origin that bears the name of the clay pot in which it is cooked. The tagine can be of many kinds. It can be fish, minced meat or kefta, chicken or tuna.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • red pepper
  • onions
  • prunes
  • garlic
  • water
  • oil
  • paprika
  • cinnamon
  • Ras el Hanout

Preparation steps

  1. Season the chicken with salt and pepper and brown it in the tagine with a splash of oil. We turn it over so that it browns well on all sides, remove and reserve
  2. Add a little more oil if necessary, and fry the chopped onion, pepper and garlic.
  3. When the vegetables are cooked, salt them, put the chicken back, add the Ras el Hanout (or the mixture of spices that you are going to use), the cinnamon and anise, approximately 2 glasses of water, cover and let cook over low heat for 20 minutes
  4. Add the plums and dried apricots, if we see that it is a little dry, add a little more hot water, stir, cover again and cook for 15 more minutes.
  5. To prepare the couscous, in a saucepan, heat the water or chicken broth. When about to boil, but before it boils, turn off the heat, add the couscous taking care that it is completely submerged in the liquid, cover and let stand for 5 minutes
  6. After this time, with a fork we shell the couscous so that it loosens, we add it to the tagine, stir and serve.
